About Canoceania

Can-Oceania is an authorized consultancy for Canadian Immigration matters based in Edmonton, Alberta. The director, Prashant Dhiman is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant.

Prashant has vast experience in different fields of occupation. As an immigrant himself he always had a keen interest in Canadian Immigration law which led him on the path to become an immigration consultant himself. He takes pride in his meticulous work and lays great value on client-consultant relationship.
